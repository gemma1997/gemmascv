Secondary Educational Software

User chooses whether to login or signup

View after student logs in.

View after teacher logs in. Teacher can take one or more quizes and can also see what each student scored on each quiz.

Student has a choice of 10 quiezes. The quizes are MCQ, True/False or Fill in the Blank. After quiz is taken users score is displayed and stored in the database.

This is an example of one of 10 quizes.

When students took quizzes, saved scores to a database with accessibility only for teachers.
